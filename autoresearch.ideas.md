Constructor stack tracking appears coupled with constructor/prototype runtime state more subtly than expected; avoid this area during perf loop without deeper debugging.

Method invocation has subtle `this`/home object/Symbol.hasInstance/prototype interactions; do not bypass `Runner.invoke_with_receiver` without narrower eligibility and dedicated tests.
- VM compiler perf: replacing Runner compiled-cache `:erlang.phash2(fun)` with a smaller selected-field hash broke cache-key expectations and `instanceof` tests. Compiled cache key shape is covered by tests and coupled to constructor/prototype behavior; do not alter without updating all cache-key helpers and semantics tests.
- VM compiler perf: `GlobalEnv.refresh/1` fast-return for nil/empty persistent globals was kept by the narrow perf/check loop, but focused compiler tests later showed it breaks `instanceof` paths. Refresh must still mark/synchronize context even when no persistent globals are present; do not keep no-op refresh skips without broader compiler tests in checks.
